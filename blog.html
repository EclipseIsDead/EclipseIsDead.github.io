09/05/20 - Project North Star Archived

The augmented reality project, initially undertaken at my school to foster more innovation in a high school setting, was cut short by corona virus. Having graduated it seems unlikely that I will be returning to continue development. Although I was unable to see the project come to fruition, the knowledge of augmented reality and Unity 3D on the LeapMotion platform will surely come in useful. And who knows, I may come back to it at a later date to develop and pick up where I left out.
